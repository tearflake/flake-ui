2.2. orbiting the content elements

T

he structure of content in FlakeUI is visualized in a distinctive way, resembling a form of a fractal arranged ovals where child ovals orbit around each parent oval recursively. Ovals themselves store textual or graphic information fitting into hierarchical parent-children relations. Dragging ovals around their parents enables walking horizontally through the tree (navigating through siblings), while dragging in or out of oval parent or children positions enables walking vertically through the hierarchical structure of data (focusing a parent or a child oval at central browsing area).
